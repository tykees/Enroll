Processes

  • Create a Steem account through Steemit. The process might take a few days to get your account verified, but you could contact us on discord to get full information for instant account.
  • Visit typeearn.com and "login as school" with the account created on Steem
  • Buy Steem Token that amount the dollar worth of the number of student you wish to upload through any exchange listed here or buy on blocktrades and send to your Steem account.
    • For Nigerians: It is recommended that you use the Binance to buy Bitcoin or Ethereum and convert to Steem Token via the Binance market, and send to your Steem acoount.
  • Login to Steem-engine and move your Steem token to Steem-Engine then buy the Typecoin(TPC).
  • Login to your https://typeearn.com/profile as a School and click the "Account" button and select "Burn TPC"
  • This will create the space to upload the number of student subscribed/burnt for.
  • On https://typeearn.com/profile (General Overview tab) scroll down to the "Add Student" button, select and choose either single or bulk upload, and upload your students.
  • Immediately, the students data will be added to the server, and they can login with their credentials (Registration number and password)
It is recommended that the students use an easy to remember password to avoid issues of forgeting password.

Reward for Schools

There is a constant 0.1 point in every typing activities of all students in a school server. This point will be accumulating in the School typeearn account and can be converted to Typecoin(TPC)based on the conversion of Typepoint(TPP) to Typecoin(TPC) rate.

At $0.06 of 1TPC, 100TPP = 10TPC. Plus, Every 100% growth of TPC will cause a 30% reduction in the conversion rate. The reduction in conversion is for stability, but still on a good side for schools.

Reward for students

  • Reward for the most active student of Typeearn in every 3 months.
  • Certificate for all student above 60 WPM (Within the pro stages.)
